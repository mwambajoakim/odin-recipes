Cherry Pie Recipe

Description

Wow your family and friends with this first-place winning pie. Get creative with the lattice design or cut out other shapes with the pie crust. And if you don't have fresh sour cherries, reviewers say you can use canned tart cherries.

This cherry pie recipe includes a buttery homemade crust and easy cherry filling. I was so pleased when this recipe got first place in the 1999 American Pie Council's National Pie Championship!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup shortening, chilled
  • ½ cup cold water
  • 2 cups pitted sour cherries
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar
  • 10 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ teaspoon almond extract
